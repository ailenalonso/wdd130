Projects

In my projects as an interior designer, I look for the client to feel comfortable and at ease with their choices, when starting with this design I understood their needs, they wanted to achieve an open space that includes the kitchen, dining room, and living room, on a functional and practical way in a reduced space. The main objective was to achieve a good separation of each sector with elements and furniture, so that the change from one sector to another can be noticed, still being an open space.

In this project, the client was looking to re-design the living room, although the space was reduced, we wanted to achieve an environment that meets the required functionality. The main objective of this project was to achieve a space that provides warmth, which could be achieved by taking the design to warm colors, and wood, and installing a fireplace.
